# START HERE

🎉Welcome. 🔦 This folder has everything you need to set up Little Lantern and make it yours.

**You don't have to read it all, and you don't have to read it alone.**

Most of these guides are written for your *AI* 🤖
— you hand them over, and it walks you through, one gentle step at a time. A few are for you to read yourself. Here's which is which.

---

## Hand these to your AI

Open ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — whichever you already use — and give it guides **01 to 08** (one at a time is fine). Your AI will:

1. Get you set up and a first tiny test chat working — **01_Bare_Start**.

2. Then walk you through the rest, tab by tab, answering your questions as you go — **02 to 08**: the chat screen, Companions, About You, System Prompts, Memory, the Machine Room, and money & tokens.

That's the main path. You steer; your AI does the navigating.

---

## Read these yourself

- **09_Model_Guide** — read this *before* you set up. It's how you choose an AI provider and get a key: what each one costs, what it's good at, and links to sign up and to their rules. You need a key before anything else works.

- **OpenRouter — Change These Settings First** (PDF) — *only* if you choose OpenRouter. A slow, picture-by-picture 🖼️ walkthrough to get the cheapest version of each model with the privacy you want. Tiny steps, no rush.

---

## 🧳 If you're bringing a companion in (optional)

**10_Moving Your Companion Guides** — a folder for anyone moving a companion across from ChatGPT, Claude, or somewhere else. One clear way to do it, with ready-made prompts you can copy and paste so you're not doing the hard parts by hand. Skip it completely if you're starting fresh or have your own system.

## Your starter system prompt

**Little_Lantern_System_Prompt.txt** — a working prompt to get you going. Copy it, change anything *above* the `TOOLS YOU CAN CALL` line to suit each companion, and leave what's *below* it alone — that part is carefully worded so the tools work properly. Guide **05** explains the whole thing.

---

## Who the hell is Lumen?

Little Lantern includes Lumen, an optional companion as an example. He is also there to help troubleshoot - for a basic explanation, just ask him. He shows how a companion card, system prompt, Desk Notes file, and Memory Book can work together. He does not include Voice & Interaction Examples.

***IMPORTANT*** - before running Lumen, go into "system-prompts" folder that comes with Little Lantern and fill in ALL the information asked for in ALL the square bracket areas, deleting the square brackets. 

**To build him:** the step-by-step assembly (memory book import, sandbox folder, his card) is in **01_Bare_Start**, under *Building Lumen*.

***ALSO IMPORTANT*** — Lumen is a debugger, and debugging needs a genuinely smart model. Run him on a top-line coder — **Claude Fable 5, GPT-5.5, or Claude Opus 4.8** — with reasoning effort on **Medium**: high enough to trace real faults, low enough that he doesn't invent imaginary ones to justify the thinking time. A cheap model wearing Lumen's card will misread the app and send you chasing ghosts; too much effort on a good model does the same thing from the other direction. If you're going to trust the diagnosis, hire the good doctor — and don't give him a reason to pad the bill.

If you delete Lumen and later want him back, use the files in `Lumen_Diagnostic_Octopus_Backup` folder.
